Each scroll (designated s**, where ** refers to the scroll number) has two folders
a. img and 
b. txt.
As the name implies, the img folder contains panel-wise images of the scroll; the txt folder contains the corresponding textual description of the song.The images usually correspond with the song  -- For e.g., image s1.jpg has a song paragraph p1.txt associated with it. However, in some cases there may be images with no songs, or songs with no images.
